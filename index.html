Common questions

Quick answers to what most first-time clients ask before booking. If anything is missing, message us on WhatsApp — Evelyn will reply personally.

Will my brows look very dark after embroidery?

Brows usually appear darker for the first few days, then soften noticeably as they heal. Evelyn will explain what to expect for your skin type and chosen style before treatment begins, so the colour you settle into is the one you actually want.

Is eyebrow embroidery painful?

Most clients describe it as much more manageable than expected — many even doze off during the session. Evelyn works gently, talks you through each step, and pauses whenever you need.

Can you fix uneven brows or previous embroidery?

Often yes. Brow mapping is used to improve visual balance, and we are happy to assess previous embroidery on WhatsApp first. The honest recommendation depends on your natural shape and how earlier work has healed.

How do I know which treatment suits me?

Send us a message on WhatsApp with your concern (and a clear photo if relevant). Evelyn will recommend the most suitable option and an estimated price — with no obligation to book.

How long does eyebrow embroidery last?

Results are semi-permanent and gradually soften over time. Lifespan varies with skin type, sun exposure and aftercare, so we walk you through realistic expectations and any recommended touch-up timing during consultation.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes — treatments are by appointment so the studio can give every client unhurried, focused attention. Message us with your preferred date and we will confirm available slots.
